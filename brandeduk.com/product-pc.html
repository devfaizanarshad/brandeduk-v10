GD067 Premium Hoodie

A perfect combination of stylish design and practical detail. This amazing 80/20 cotton blend hoodie has a 100% cotton face so that it prints perfectly every time. Ideal for DTG, screen printing, discharge and embroidery.

Two-end ringspun cotton face fleece. Two-piece Jersey-lined hood. Dropped shoulder. Pouch pocket. 1x1 rib with Spandex cuffs and waistband for enhanced stretch and recovery. Classic fit, tubular body. Tear-away label.

Fabric: 80% Ringspun Cotton / 20% Polyester
Weight: 285gsm (White 278gsm)
Washing: Machine wash 30C. Do not bleach. Low tumble. Do not iron. No dry clean.
